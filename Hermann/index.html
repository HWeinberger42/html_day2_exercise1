Weekend Activity Planner for Vienna

Day Morning Afternoon
Saturday
KHM

Kunsthistorisches Museum is one of the most popular museums in the world, its rich holdings comprise objects from five millennia, from Ancient Egypt to the late eighteenth century.
Aqua Terra Zoo
Originally an anti-aircraft tower from World War II, it is now a zoo with over 10,000 animals resulting from almost 7 decades of dedicated scientific work. Here you can admire a variety of animal species on 11 floors: sharks, rays, turtles on water and on land, lizards of all kinds, snakes, salt and freshwater fish, birds, flying foxes, various monkeys, insects, and a whole lot more…

  2. Secession
    • the world’s oldest independent exhibition institution specifically dedicated to contemporary art
    • designed by Joseph Maria Olbrich and the works of the association’s founding members of the Secession were instrumental in defining the Viennese brand of Jugendstil
    • open venue for presentations of new creative ideas and engagements with the urgent concerns of our time
    • motto: To each era its art. To art its freedom

  2. Vienna State Opera
    • The structure of the opera house was planned by the Viennese architect August Sicard von Sicardsburg, while the inside was designed by interior decorator Eduard van der Nüll.
    • On May 25, 1869, the opera house solemnly opened with Mozart's »Don Giovanni«
    • On November 5, 1955, the Vienna State Opera reopened with a new auditorium and modernized technology.
    • It has been under the direction of Bogdan Roščić since July 1, 2020.
