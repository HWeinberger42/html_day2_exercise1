KHM

Kunsthistorisches Museum is one of the most popular museums in the world, its rich holdings comprise objects from five millennia, from Ancient Egypt to the late eighteenth century.
Aqua Terra Zoo
Originally an anti-aircraft tower from World War II, it is now a zoo with over 10,000 animals resulting from almost 7 decades of dedicated scientific work.
